// FAQ

The questions parents usually ask.

Does my kid need any experience?

No. We start from scratch — Tinkercad is built for beginners and Viaan walks every kid through it personally.

What does my child take home?

Their own 3D print, 2–3 DF3D stickers, a Vox mascot card, a filament sample, and a 5% off code for the shop. They also get a "bring a friend to the next workshop — you both save 15%" card. If their design takes longer than the session, we post it to you within the week — included in the price.

Do parents need to stay?

Totally up to you. Parents who stay are welcome to sit with their kid and help — it's a nice way to do it together. Otherwise just drop off and pick up at the end.

What if the print isn't finished by the end?

That's fine. Every kid also goes home with a pre-made DF3D keychain so no one leaves empty-handed, and we'll post the in-progress print to you that week.

What should my kid bring?

If possible, bring a laptop — kids design best on a screen they're already used to. If you can't, no worries: we'll have laptops ready to use. Everything else (printer, filament, design help, snacks, take-home bag) is on us. If they have a favourite reference image or sketch they want to turn into a 3D print, bring that too.

What about allergies and snacks?

We keep snacks simple — juice boxes, fruit, and dry cookies. Let us know any allergies at signup and we'll work around them.

How do I pay?

Fill in the booking form below and we'll email you a payment link (PayNow or Stripe). Your spot is held for 24 hours while you confirm.
