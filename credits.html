// MODELS · DESIGNERS · COMMUNITY

Dragonfruit 3D is a small-batch 3D printing studio. We print on demand using a mix of community-designed models from the maker community and our own work. This page exists to credit the designers whose work we sell, in line with the licensing terms of platforms like MakerWorld, Printables, and Thingiverse.

We're committed to giving credit where it's due. If you're a designer and recognize your work here — whether it's already credited or not — please reach out so we can attribute it correctly (or remove it on request).
